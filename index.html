Welcome to Student-Senior Tech Connections

We the Tech Savvy Teens, or TEST want to help pair today's teens with senior citizens who are having technological problems, in order to better their lives. Many elderly people have trouble with todayâ€™s technology and it can be frustrating and troublesome. This can affect them in many ways, like making it difficult for them to reach out to family and friends, and making them more susceptible to scamming and malicious attacks. This program will help the teens gain community service hours by meeting with our senior citizens to help them better understand how to use technology, or to fix their technological issues. We understand that some senior citizens will find it difficult to use our application, to counter this we will implement other ways of reaching out to them, or those close to them. Our application will work with high schools and senior citizen centers to find students who are willing and eager to help senior citizens.
